The Best Movies According to Anthony

My top 3 movies of all time

Dead Silence

This is an underated movie that I truly. Its creative use of dolls in this movie created a creepy atmosphere

Monsters' Inc

This movie was my favorite movie as a child. The story was entertaining and the monsters are creative. My favorite character is Sully

Dinner for Schmucks

This movie is one of the funniest movies I have ever seen. Steve Carrell is incredible in this movie
